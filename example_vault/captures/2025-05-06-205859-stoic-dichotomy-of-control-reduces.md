title: Stoic Dichotomy of Control Reduces Anxiety
updated: '2025-05-06T20:58:59+00:00'
---

Focusing only on what you can control (your actions, reactions) and accepting what you cannot (others' behavior, outcomes) reduces pointless anxiety.
